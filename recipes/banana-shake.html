Banana Shake

Description

A delicious banana shake to try if you don't have a lot of time or ingredients! Cinnamon and sugar can be omitted.
it is healthy and tasty. you can also put more things in it like icecream, chocklates to make it more tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 banana
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine ice, milk, banana, sugar, and cinnamon in a blender; blend until smooth.
  3. Add some ice and milk if shake is not smooth and blend it again
  4. put into glasses to serve
