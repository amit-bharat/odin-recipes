Mango Shake

Description

his delicious and healthy shake can conquer any sweet tooth without guilt. Great for extinguishing the fire in your mouth after eating hot curries!
Use Indian mangoes, preferably Alphonso or another sweet Indian cultivar. These are the orange ones, not the red.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain whole milk yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 mangoes - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 3 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place the yogurt, milk, mangoes, white sugar, and ice into the jar of a blender
  2. blend until smooth, about 2 minutes
  3. serve sprinkled with a little ground cardamom
